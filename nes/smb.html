Super Mario Bros.

Super Mario Bros. is a side-scrolling platform video game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), featuring Mario and Luigi as they embark on a journey to rescue Princess Toadstool from the villainous Bowser, popularizing the platforming genre and becoming one of the most influential and beloved video games of all time.
